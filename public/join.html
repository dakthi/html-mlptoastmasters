Easy membership renewal

Renewing your membership is quick and simple:

  1. Login to Toastmasters.org (not EasySpeak)
  2. Click on 'Self-pay'
  3. Complete the payment process

The system will show if you need to renew. Membership periods run for six months, with most renewals happening in September/March.
