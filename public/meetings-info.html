Our meetings are designed to help you develop your communication and leadership skills in a supportive and encouraging environment. Whether you're a first-time guest or a seasoned member, you'll find valuable opportunities to learn and grow.

What happens:

  • Prepared speeches by members working on their Pathways projects
  • Table Topics session for impromptu speaking practice
  • Constructive evaluations to help speakers improve
  • Role opportunities to develop leadership skills
  • Networking and fellowship with fellow members
  • Business updates and club announcements

What happens:

  • International Speech Contest (spring)
  • Evaluation & Table Topics Contests
  • Humorous Speech Contest (fall)
  • Club officer installation ceremonies
  • Social events and team-building activities
  • Guest speaker sessions and workshops

What happens:

  • Full participation via Zoom video conferencing
  • Same meeting format as in-person meetings
  • Interactive sessions with chat and reactions
  • Screen sharing for presentations and materials
  • Breakout rooms for group activities
  • Recorded sessions available for review

Who should attend:

All members and guests are welcome to attend our regular meetings. This is the core of our club activities where you'll practice speaking, receive feedback, and build your confidence in a supportive environment.

Who should attend:

Members and invited guests are encouraged to attend special events. Contests are great opportunities to challenge yourself, while social events help build club camaraderie and celebrate achievements.

Who should attend:

Anyone who can't attend in-person can join our meetings online. Perfect for members with busy schedules, those traveling, or guests who want to experience Toastmasters from anywhere.

First Time Visiting?

We welcome guests to experience our meetings! No preparation is needed - just come and observe. You can participate as much or as little as you're comfortable with. Contact us to RSVP or ask any questions about what to expect.
