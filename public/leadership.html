About Club Leadership

The executive committee provides the strategic direction and operational management of our club. These leadership roles offer invaluable experience in management, team coordination, and organizational leadership.

You Don't Need Experience to Lead

"Don't need to be an experienced Toastmaster to stand for roles. Just need curiosity, interest, time, and conviction."

Real Story: At our December 2, 2025 meeting, Amr attended his 2nd meeting ever and was elected Vice President of Membership! He said: "Public speaking is a skill that improves over time, not overnight. You have to come and keep improving."

Just weeks later, he's now leading our membership efforts and welcoming new guests to the club.

Featured Leadership Opportunities

Vice President of Membership

What this role does:

  • Welcome guests and potential members at club meetings
  • Coordinate guest visits and follow up with interested individuals
  • Monitor member participation and engagement levels
  • Organize member appreciation activities and recognition

Skills you'll develop:

Relationship building, sales and persuasion, customer service, organization and planning, strategic thinking, empathy and listening

Vice President of Public Relations

What this role does:

  • Create and schedule engaging social media posts
  • Manage club presence on Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram
  • Write compelling posts about club meetings and member achievements
  • Create graphics, videos, and other visual content

Skills you'll develop:

Social media marketing, content creation and copywriting, digital marketing, brand management, analytics and tracking
